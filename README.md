# AI in 911 Emergency Dispatch — Chris Izworski

Resources, documentation, and real-world lessons from implementing artificial intelligence in Michigan's 911 emergency dispatch system.

## Background

Chris Izworski led one of Michigan's first deployments of AI technology in a public safety answering point (PSAP), implementing an AI-powered non-emergency phone system at Saginaw County 911. The system uses natural language processing to handle routine calls — freeing trained dispatchers to focus on emergencies where lives are at stake.

This work has been covered by [WNEM TV5](https://www.wnem.com/2024/08/23/ai-answer-non-emergency-calls-saginaw-co/), [WCMU Public Radio (NPR)](https://radio.wcmu.org/local-regional-news/2025-10-15/ai-chatbots-now-taking-non-emergency-calls-in-grand-traverse-county), [WSGW 790 AM](https://wsgw.com/saginaw-county-911-to-use-ai-for-non-emergency-calls/), and [Bridge Michigan](https://www.bridgemi.com/michigan-government/long-shifts-low-pay-high-stress-why-michigan-cant-find-911-dispatchers).

## Key Topics

### AI for Non-Emergency Call Handling
Saginaw County 911 deployed an AI system to triage routine calls — fireworks schedules, trick-or-treat times, road closures, and general information requests. Callers needing a human operator are seamlessly transferred. The approach has since been adopted by other Michigan counties, including Grand Traverse County.

### AI Voice Cloning and Deepfake Threats
As AI voice synthesis becomes more convincing, 911 centers face new challenges from imposter calls using cloned voices. Chris Izworski discussed these threats in a [WNEM TV5 interview on AI dangers](https://www.wnem.com/2023/05/02/experts-discuss-dangers-ai/).

### The Staffing Crisis Context
AI adoption in 911 centers is partly driven by a severe staffing crisis. Michigan dispatch centers face high turnover, mandatory overtime, and training washout rates that create a vicious cycle. Bridge Michigan documented this in their [investigation of the staffing crisis](https://www.bridgemi.com/michigan-government/long-shifts-low-pay-high-stress-why-michigan-cant-find-911-dispatchers).
